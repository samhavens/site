About

I am currently an AI researcher on the NLP team at MosaicML, working under Jonathan Frankle. We focus on empirical methods to speed up model convergence.

Previously I led NLP at Writer. Prior to that I was the CTO of the conversational AI startup CarLabs.

I like to build innovative products using machine learning, as well as understand, empirically, how these models work. I try to create a supportive environment in which people can learn and create work that they are proud of.

I volunteer in the education sector. For fun, I lift weights, run, learn, and go exploring with my family.

Advising

I occasionally advise investors, founders, and executives about NLP and generative models. If you would like to learn more about modern NLP and its current and expected future capabilities, reach out by email or Twitter.
